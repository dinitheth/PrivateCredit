# CreditVault - Project Description

## What is CreditVault?

CreditVault is a privacy-first decentralized lending platform that reimagines how credit scoring works. Instead of handing over your sensitive financial data to a centralized institution, CreditVault lets you keep your information completely private—encrypted on your device—while still getting instant credit assessments and loan opportunities.

Think of it as a credit evaluation system that never actually sees your financial data. Your salary, debts, and expenses stay encrypted the entire time. The system computes your credit score directly on the encrypted data using advanced mathematical techniques, so no human (or institution) ever reads your private information. It's like having a credit evaluation done in a locked vault that only you hold the key to.

## The Problem We're Solving

Today's credit scoring system feels broken:

**Privacy Invasion**
When you apply for a loan, you have to share your complete financial picture—your salary, your debts, your monthly expenses—with banks, lenders, and third parties. This information gets stored on dozens of servers. One data breach, and your entire financial life is exposed. You have zero control over who sees what.

**Centralized Control**
Credit bureaus act as gatekeepers. They decide your creditworthiness based on their proprietary algorithms. You can't challenge their computations. You can't verify they're fair. You just have to trust them.

**Data Leakage Risk**
The more places your financial data lives, the more opportunities for it to leak. Data breaches happen constantly. Your sensitive information is sitting in multiple databases, each a potential target.

**Limited Access**
If you want to apply for multiple loans or compare offers, you have to go through the same data-sharing process each time. Your information gets copied around even more.

## How CreditVault Works

CreditVault uses cutting-edge cryptography called Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE) to solve this. Here's what makes it different:

**You Control Your Data**
1. You enter your financial information (salary, debts, expenses) into CreditVault
2. Your browser immediately encrypts this data using advanced encryption keys that only you control
3. The encrypted data is sent to the blockchain—no unencrypted version ever leaves your device
4. Only you can decrypt the results with your private key

**Computation on Encrypted Data**
Instead of decrypting your information so the system can evaluate it (which would expose it), CreditVault performs all calculations *directly* on the encrypted data. It's like doing math on a locked safe without ever opening it.

- We calculate your debt-to-income ratio on encrypted data
- We compute your expense ratios on encrypted data
- We generate your credit score on encrypted data
- The system never sees the actual numbers—only the encrypted versions

**Transparent Results**
You get a credit score in the standard 300-850 range. Lenders see a risk assessment (low/medium/high risk) but never see your raw financial data. Everyone gets what they need without exposing what they shouldn't.

**Verified on Blockchain**
All computations happen on Ethereum Sepolia with cryptographic proof. Nothing happens in a black box. You can verify that your credit score was computed fairly and hasn't been tampered with.
